Uninstall the inductiva package

Uninstall the inductiva package#

Whether you just want a completely new installation of inductiva or simply want to completely remove the package from your system, we’ve got you covered. Either way, we would like to give you instructions on how to completely remove any trace of inductiva from your system.

NOTE No simulation files generated through the package will be removed.

First, you should uninstall the package using:

pip uninstall inductiva

Next, recall that the inductiva package, writes locally to your disk. Therefore, you should have a folder under $HOME/.inductiva in your system. Remove it and you are good to go!

rm -rf $HOME/.inductiva
